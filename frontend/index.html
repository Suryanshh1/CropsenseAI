About the Project

What is CropSense AI?

CropSense AI is an artificial intelligence platform developed to address two of the most critical challenges in modern agriculture: the early detection of plant diseases and the accurate forecasting of crop yield before harvest season.

The platform provides farmers, agronomists, and agricultural organizations with actionable intelligence — turning a leaf photograph or a set of field parameters into precise, data-driven recommendations that protect crops and optimize production planning.

The Problem Being Solved: Crop diseases cause an estimated 20–40% of global agricultural losses each year. Farmers often lack timely access to expert diagnosis. Traditional yield estimation is inaccurate and slow — resulting in poor planning and economic uncertainty for millions of farming households.
01

Vision-Based Disease Detection

A deep learning model trained on thousands of plant leaf images identifies disease patterns invisible to the naked eye — in seconds, without requiring laboratory testing or specialist consultation.

02

Environmental Yield Forecasting

A machine learning regression model uses local field conditions — rainfall, temperature, and pesticide usage — to predict crop output in hectogram-per-hectare units before harvest.

03

Accessible REST API Infrastructure

Both models are served through a lightweight API, making integration with mobile applications, IoT sensors, and farm management systems straightforward and scalable.

Process

How the Platform Works

The platform operates through two parallel AI pipelines — one for visual crop disease diagnosis, and one for numerical yield prediction. Both are accessible through this web interface and a REST API endpoint for integration into existing systems.
Step 01

Upload a Leaf Image

Photograph any affected plant leaf and upload it to the platform. The system accepts standard image formats and automatically resizes and normalizes the image for analysis.

Step 02

AI Vision Analysis

A deep learning neural network scans the visual texture and color patterns of the leaf, comparing them against trained disease profiles for Potato and Tomato crop families.

Step 03

Instant Diagnosis

Receive a precise classification — Potato Early Blight, Tomato Early Blight, Tomato Late Blight, or Healthy — with actionable treatment guidance when disease is detected.

Step 04

Enter Field Conditions

Input your local agricultural parameters: annual rainfall in millimeters, pesticide tonnage applied, and average seasonal temperature in degrees Celsius.

Step 05

Predictive Yield Model

An ensemble of decision trees analyzes the relationship between your field conditions and historical yield data to produce a calibrated harvest estimate.

Step 06

Yield Forecast Report

Receive a predicted yield in hectograms per hectare with a per-tonne equivalent — ready for use in harvest planning, procurement, and supply chain decisions.

"Empowering every farmer with the same intelligence that was once only available to large agricultural enterprises and well-funded research institutions."

The core mission of CropSense AI is the democratization of agricultural intelligence. High-quality crop diagnosis and yield forecasting have historically required expensive consultants, laboratory facilities, and specialized equipment — resources unavailable to small and medium-scale farmers who produce the majority of the world's food supply.


By delivering AI-powered analysis through a simple image upload and a web form, CropSense AI puts expert-level insight directly into the hands of farmers, extension officers, and agricultural co-operatives — regardless of their location or technical background.
